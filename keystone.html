    DESCRIPTION

    This project is the work of VR&Story telling Collab in Parsons, lead by Anezka. We as the Parsons design student, corporate with the middle school students in New Jersy and make a game together.

    During the class, mid school students, Milo and Becket is respond to the story script and concept. Me and Teayeon, the parsons students, is respond to make it to a palyable VR game. The final work is exhibited in the Parsons LCCS Show.

    This story is a fantasy called Keystone City of Portal, about Alex who wants to get home, despite having to find parts of very old stone key, guarded jealously by a monster. Keystone is a Magical city of doors and portals to everywhere.

    本作品是2017年由我校Anezka教授带领的VR&Story Telling课程作品。本课程意在通过和中学生的合作，发掘VR游戏的可能性。在此合作中，我们的中学生搭档 Milo和 Becket负责故事脚本和角色设计。我和我的搭档具体负责概念的实现和优化。最终成果在Parsons LSSC show 上进行了展示。

    本游戏的背景是一位名叫Alex的儿童，在不幸的家庭中，为填补心中的空缺而进行的奇幻冒险故事。在冒险的过程中，Alex必须要经历过重重考验，寻找能够解救巨大机器人和洞穴女妖的钥匙，以此拯救即将破碎的传送门之城。
