    DESCRIPTION

    Shakerama is a web based game that inspired by my long-distance relationship with my girlfriend. I want to make a game that can be played together with she and eventhough she is working in the office. By this way, I hope it can strengthen our relationship.

    The technology I'm using mostly a javascript library called "phaser". By this library, I can easily build up an online game and connect with my bluetooth device.

    By shaking the leg, the vibration detector sends signal to the service and players need to adjust the frequency of shaking leg to achieve a higher score.

    本游戏的灵感来源于我与女友的异地关系，我希望通过制作这样一款网页游戏，即使对方没有游戏平台，也能轻松地一起玩游戏。我希望通过这样一款游戏，加深我和女友的异地关系。

    在此作品中，我主要采用了名为“phaser”的js库，通过此工具搭建起了基础的游戏机制。而角色设计的灵感来源于“千与千寻”中的煤球精灵，因为煤球精灵不会说话，他们只能通过一些动作来传达感情，我觉得这与本游戏有着异曲同工之妙
