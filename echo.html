    ECHO

    FIELDS

    Soundscape / Physicial computing

    CONTEXT

    YEAR

    2018

    TOOLS

    DESIGN
    DESCRIPTION

    My art project is a serial of experiments which show the process of how I de-romanticizing myself. By exploring the Hyperobject,I am provoking that romanticism is dangerous, also suggesting a new way of how we coexisting with non-human in the future.

    I’m making a soundscape art project that reproduces the sound of rain in bamboo forest with artificial products such as PVC pipes, plastic bags and solenoids. I want the audience to experience this artificial non-musical sound performance in a space that is constructed of multiple artificial materials.

    Through this installation, I’m showing that romanticizing the nature is actually a problematic way on how we think about nature. I continue to question which one is more truthful, nature or non-nature.

    我的作品“Echo” 的灵感来源于我本科时的乡村题材项目“寻乡”。在“寻乡”项目过程中，乡村的声音带给了我启发，当再次回到城市时，我试图通过运用一些代表环境污染的一次性塑料袋、步进马达、塑料颗粒等人工材料来试图重现山林初春的雨声。

    “Echo” 所发出的声音与其来源看似矛盾，但当我们将人类所带来的对坏境的破坏和污染放在亿万年的地球维度中时，人类的一切行为都成为了地球生态的一小部分。人类早已习惯试图将自然改造成自己所希望的样子，“Echo”正试图改变大众对于生态的固有印象，提醒我们直面环境污染带来的生态改变。
